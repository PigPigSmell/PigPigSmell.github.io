Research

HoloSpace: Mixed-Reality Telepresence for Flexible Navigational Remote Collaboration

HoloSpace is a system that enables on-demand, unbounded mixed-reality telepresence to support navigational remote collaboration. The mixed-reality telepresence offered by HoloSpace is a fusion of dependent perspective and independent perspective approaches, which enables a local user’s immediate request of remote assistance on a novel site, as well as the remote user’s flexible navigation with the local user in unbound space. To validate the system, we conducted an exploratory study with twelve participants who cooperate remotely on a navigational task. Based on the results and user feedback, we conclude the benefits of HoloSpace and discuss directions for future development. (Poster is in the portfoli)
